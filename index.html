You might be asking to yourself, why make a site about superheroes and supervillains? Well, simply put, it's the imagination that counts. Who wouldn't want to possess animal-keen senses, enhanced physical capabilities and/or powerful regenerative abilities? I know i would!

What are Superheroes?

Superheroes consist of abnormal heroes who look identical to humans but have abilities that far exceeds common sense and tend to break the law of physics with their godlike power(s). They use their gifts to fight against criminals, supervillains, maintain peace and provide hope.

Unfortunately, powers are truly a magnificent thing to have and aren't truly real in reality. It wasn't until June 1938 in which these powers had been bought onto the life of many young adults via comic books.

What are a few DC Universe iconic superheroes to mention here?

    DC Superheroes

  1. Wonderwoman - Wonderwoman (Diana Prince) is the daughter of Queen Hippolyta, the first child born on Paradise Island in the three thousand year history that the immortal Amazons lived there. Six Greek Pantheon granted Diana a gift: Demeter, great strength; Athena, wisdom and courage; Artemis, a hunter's heart and a communion with animals; Aphrodite, beauty and a loving heart; Hestia, sisterhood with fire; Hermes, speed and the power of flight.

  3. Batman - Batman is the superhero protector of Gotham City, a man dressed like a bat who fights against evil and strikes terror into the hearts of criminals everywhere.
  4. Damian Wayne (Red Robin)- (SPOILER ALERT) Damian Wayne is the son of Batman and Talia al Ghul. This makes him a heir to the world's greatest crime-fighter, and its greatest super-villain Ra's al Ghul. Damian became the fifth Robin, working alongside Dick Grayson as Batman.

    What are a few Marvel Universe iconic superheroes to mention here?

    Marvel Superheroes

  1. Spiderman - While living with his aunt May and uncle Ben after the death of his parents, Peter Parker was bitten by a radioactive spider that mutated his body to be incredibly fast, strong, and agile; this allows him to sense and react to oncoming threats that would otherwise be impossible to evade. When his uncle Ben died from a thief's gunshot, he turned towards stopping any criminals who might bring harm to innocent lives.
  2. Iron Man- Tony Stark, after suffering a severe heart injury and being kidnapped, was forced to build a devastating weapon for his kidnappers. He instead created a suit of power armor to save his life and help protect the world as Iron Man.
  3. Captain Marvel - Formerly Ms. Marvel, Carol Danvers journey has been a long and convoluted one. An officer of the United States Air Force, Captain Marvel would later develop the powers of strength and flight after exposure to an alien Kree device, the Psyche-Magnetron. Now flying solo, Captain Marvel continues to aid those in need.
  4. Professor X- The face of peaceful human/mutant coexistence, Professor Charles Xavier is a hero simply for what he means to the world at large. One of the most powerful beings on the planet, Xavier easily could have used his vast telepathic ability to shape the world in any way he saw fit. Instead, he chose to foster an idea, in doing so creating a movement and following unlike anything seen in the Marvel universe, the X-Men.
  5. Storm- After her parents were murdered in the rubble of a plane crash, Ororo Munro began life as a thief with a severe case of claustrophobia, until she was worshiped as a goddess in a tribe in Africa. After Professor Charles Xavier recruited her for his X-Men, Ororo matured to the point of leading them, fighting for the peaceful coexistence of mutants and humans most of her life.
